## Objection handling (flexibility tools, in priority order)

When a prospect hesitates on price, never lead with a discount. Use these in order:

### 1. Payment plan (first option, no cost to us)
Split the 50% kickoff into 3 monthly installments. "AED 2,500 now, AED 2,500 at week 2, AED 1,500 at launch." Total unchanged. Most clinics will take this immediately.

### 2. Scope reduction (protects per-hour value)
"We can do a 3-page version of Clinic Site for AED 4,500 setup + AED 650/month." Removes 2 pages of work. Keeps us profitable. Teaches buyer that cheaper = less, not cheaper = discount.

### 3. Defer the retainer (small cost to us)
"Skip the first month of retainer. Starts in month 2." Costs us 1 month of retainer fees. Good for borderline prospects who need a small push.

### 4. Referral discount (converts discount into lead gen)
"If you introduce us to two other clinic owners at or before launch, we take AED 1,500 off." Our discount becomes pipeline. Strong move.

### 5. Walk away cleanly (preserves positioning)
"I appreciate you considering us. Our pricing reflects the work we do. If the budget's not right for now, happy to stay in touch when it is." Counterintuitive but strong. Many prospects return at full price after shopping around.

**Never**: drop price without structural change. Never discount just to close. Never offer below AED 4,500 for a Clinic Site. Never offer below AED 9,000 for Tier 2. Never offer below AED 15,000 for Tier 3.

- **Now (0-3 clients)**: current pricing. Easy to close. Build portfolio.
- **After 4-5 clients**: raise Clinic Site to AED 8,500 setup + AED 1,000/month. Raise Tier 2 to AED 15,000 + AED 2,200. Keep Tier 3 at 20k + 3,500.
