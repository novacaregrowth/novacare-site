## Channel priority (first 90 days)

In strict priority order. Do not skip higher-priority channels to chase lower ones.

### 1. Borrowed audience from Alia (highest ROI available)

Most of Novacare's first 5 clients will likely come from this.

Tactics:
- **Ask Alia to post a single story or reel tagging Novacare** when her site goes live. Write the caption for her. 1-3% of her 120k+ followers = 1,200-3,600 business-adjacent eyes on us.
- **Scrape Alia's followers list using Apify** (~AED 20-50). Filter for business accounts, wellness professionals, clinic owners.
- **Warm-bridge DMs**: message scraped followers with a line like "Hey [name], saw you follow Alia Bahri, we just launched her new website. Wanted to reach out because [specific thing about their clinic]."
- **Ask Alia for one warm intro** to another wellness business owner she respects. Make it easy: write the intro message for her to forward.
- **Co-create content** with Alia: a reel of her walking through her site and saying why she chose us. She gets content, we get 120k reach.

### 2. In-person clinic walk-ins (Dubai)

Massively underrated in UAE because the culture rewards face-to-face.

- Target areas: DHCC, JLT, Al Barsha, Business Bay, Jumeirah
- Cadence: 2 afternoons/week, 5 clinics/afternoon
- Approach: walk in, ask for the owner by name (do 5 min of research first), leave a business card + a 1-page printout showing Alia's site.
- Script: "Hi, I'm Yazan from Novacare. We just built the website for a therapist at Al Yasmine Center and I'm stopping by a few clinics in the area to show our work. Do you have 2 minutes?"
- Conversion rate target: 1 in 20 walk-ins → a real conversation. 1 in 100 → a client.

### 3. LinkedIn Loom teardowns

Builds authority publicly. Generates inbound.

- Record 5-min Loom reviewing a UAE clinic's website (pick ones you're not actively pitching)
- Post on LinkedIn + Instagram: "I looked at [clinic name]'s site. Here are 5 things I'd change that would double their bookings."
- Cadence: 2-3 per week
- Outcome: clinic owners DM saying "can you audit mine?" — that's your warm pipeline.

### 4. Warm-bridge Instagram DMs

- Cadence: 15-20 DMs/day on average
- Personalization required: reference something specific from their grid, comment on a recent post.
- Use the Alia connection when possible: "Hey [name], we just built the site for Alia Bahri's clinic. Wanted to reach out because [specific reason]."
- Track: send date, clinic name, response status. Use Airtable (free tier).

### 5. Partnership outreach

- Target: UAE clinic accountants, DHA licensing consultants, medical equipment suppliers, business setup consultants
- Goal: referral partnerships. 10-15% of setup fee + 10% of first 6 months retainer.
- Cadence: 10 conversations/month.
- One partnership that sends 1 client/quarter is worth 100 cold DMs.

### 6. SEO content (start month 2)

- Target keywords: "clinic website UAE," "DHA compliant website," "patient booking system Dubai," "therapy website UAE"
- Format: long-form articles on novacaregrowth.com/insights (or /journal)
- Cadence: 2 articles/month, drafted by Claude with heavy human editing
- Outcome: compound asset. Won't produce leads for 3-6 months but compounds thereafter.

### 7. Cold email (lowest priority)

- Cadence: 20-30/day via Instantly or Smartlead
- For UAE B2B, email is the weakest channel. Good volume, low reply rate (2-4%).
- Use as a backup channel when IG DMs aren't landing.

### 8. Meta ads (DO NOT START before month 3)

Only after we have:
- At least 500 website visitors/month from organic sources
- A lead magnet or audit offer
- A second case study beyond Alia

Then: 30 AED/day, split:
- 15 AED/day retargeting website visitors + IG engagers
- 15 AED/day boosting best-performing organic posts

Realistic outcome: 2-5 additional qualified conversations per month. Not a primary channel.

## Content plan

### Platforms

**Instagram** and **LinkedIn** only. Skip TikTok and Facebook for now.

### Instagram cadence

- 4 feed posts/week
- 3-5 stories/day
- 1 Reel/week (Reels drive 3-5x reach vs. feed)

### LinkedIn cadence

- 3 text posts/week (Yazan's personal profile, not company page)
- 1 carousel/week (company page)

### Content pillars

Rotate through these:

1. **Before/after proof** — automation demos, screen recordings of AI agents, dashboard screenshots, speed comparisons
2. **Education** — clinic marketing and tech tips for UAE. "3 things every UAE clinic website is missing," "Why your booking flow is losing you patients"
3. **Behind the scenes** — building, debugging, shipping, founder faces (optional but humanizing)
4. **Client wins** — Alia first, then future clients
5. **UAE-specific angles** — Ramadan campaigns for clinics, DHA compliance tips, bilingual automation, summer slowdowns

### Content production workflow

Use the AI content repurposing workflow (build this in week 2):

1. Yazan records a 5-min voice note or Loom explaining an idea
2. Whisper transcribes
3. Claude generates: 1 IG caption, 1 LinkedIn post, 3 story hooks, 1 Reel script
4. Yazan reviews, edits, approves
5. Buffer or Make posts on schedule

Time saved: 3 hours/day → 45 min/day of ideation and review.

## Experiments to try (after 90 days)

- **Productized micro-offer**: "AI WhatsApp reception bot for clinics — AED 3,500, live in 5 days." Foot-in-the-door offer that upsells to Tier 2.
- **Newsletter**: monthly email to UAE clinic owners with 2-3 actionable tips. Start when list hits 100.

Don't run any of these until the first 90 days are executed. Focus is the constraint.
